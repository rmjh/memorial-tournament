How to read this. It's a double-elimination tournament: two losses and you're out. A win sends Memorial deeper into the winners' bracket; a loss drops them into the elimination bracket, where one more loss ends their run. Each row below is a game Memorial could reach — read the right-hand column to see what makes it happen. Times are scheduled start times and can run early or late depending on the previous game, weather, field prep, and umpires.
